About CSE WORLD


CSE WORLD is an education website. We're committed to building the best learning experience inside and out, making CSE WORLD the best place for our team to learn, teach, and create the online learning experience of the future.

Education is old. The current public school system in the World dates back to the 19th century and wasn't designed to scale the way it has. Lots of companies are working to "disrupt" education by changing the way things work in the classroom and by bringing the classroom online.

Our Mission


We're not one of those websites. We are rethinking education from the bottom up. The web has rethought nearly everything - commerce, social networking, healthcare, and more.


We are building the education the world needs - the first truly net native education. We designed in creating an engaging educational experience than we do from the classroom.
